iRoo

In 2019, I was approached by iRoo to create a package of digital marketing design templates, which included website and social content assets. iRoo is a contemporary ready-to-wear label based in Taiwan that aims to offer a minimalist wardrobe. Its elegant design detail has made its way onto various luxury online retail websites and Physical storefronts.
